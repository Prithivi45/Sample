About Alcor

Alcor Engineering Services is a company which is offering the Engineering solutions and advanced Engineering services for Automotive, Mechanical and various Industrial sectors. We help companies to create tomorrow’s technology today by supporting them in their most complex and challenging engineering innovations. In today’s economy which is growing in a lightning fast pace, we provide timely solutions for the engineering problems faced by our clients, thus helping them be in the frontline of the competition.

We also help develop talented minds that can face the demands of today’s complex engineering problems through our state of the art training methods. Our professional development courses help students maximize their knowledge and assist in taking their career to the next level. Designed to fit into your busy schedule, these courses are conducted by some of the best-in-class trainers.
