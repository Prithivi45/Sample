Alcor offers Design and drafting services as well to our clients. Our Manufacturing Drawings are produced as per IS standards or the standards specified by our clients. At Alcor, we have delivered diverse and complex design projects to manufacturers, engineering firms and SMEs. With length and breadth of experience and expertise in CAD tools, we have served as an extended design arm to assist design engineers in shortening product development time.

CAD Projects

We have become very involved in the Engineering Design rush since 2015 for several clients involved in the industry wide Cad Services in various regions throughout the world. Our team is fully immersed into the project and understand the industry standards for the work and what is required to get them done. These clients came to Cad sourcing because of our experience in the Engineering Design and our ability to scale up for large scale projects. We have the staffing to complete these projects and the management team to make sure it is done smoothly and on schedule.

The Alcor CAD services team is led by a team of qualified Engineers who manage all projects from requirement gathering through to delivery. We support our clients with Mechanical drafting services, Mechanical detailing services and New Product Design and Development.
