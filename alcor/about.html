Welcome to Alcor

Alcor Engineering Services was founded in 2015 by the few individuals who believe that they can make a difference in the field of engineering services. It is with immense pride that we introduce ourselves to the fast growing Engineering solutions community. Our aim is to cater the needs of engineering industries by providing our clients with prompt and quality products at competitive rates and compromised quality.

Our core strength lies in the knowledge base provided by our vastly experienced professionals who can provide the right platform for the industries to fix the problems as and when they arise. With thorough expertise and knowledge in CAD field we assure our clients that we deliver on our promises by integrating our skill sets, experience, products, tools and practices.

Alcor Engineering has:

  • Engineering Services
  • Training Services.


Vision

Our vision is to be an outstanding Engineering solutions provider and also want to make Alcor Engineering become Engineering service provider Globally in the fields of Automotive, Aerospace and Mechanical.

Mission

Our mission is to provide CAD services by consistently exceeding the expectations of our customers, providing cost-effective, timely assistance for our clients and competent Engineering design, Drafting, Analyzing and manufacturing solutions.
